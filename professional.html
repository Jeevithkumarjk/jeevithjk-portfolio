Industry Engagement

Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) – Infrastructure & Mobility Systems Tour

Los Angeles, California

  • Visited one of the world’s busiest airports to study large-scale airport infrastructure and mobility transformation projects in progress at LAX.
  • ConRAC (Consolidated Rent-A-Car Facility): Observed the development of one of the largest rental car facilities globally, designed to centralize rental operations and directly connect with the Automated People Mover (APM) to reduce roadway congestion and improve passenger experience.
  • Automated People Mover (APM): Studied the elevated APM system forming a critical part of one of the largest airport mobility transformations in the U.S., providing seamless connections between terminals, parking, Metro rail, and ConRAC.
  • Terminal Extension & Modernization: Learned about advanced construction strategies used to expand and modernize terminal facilities, including lifting, shifting, and extending structures while maintaining active airport operations.
  • Aircraft Spotting & Runway Operations: Observed aircraft movements on one of the busiest airfields in the world and gained insight into how airside operations are coordinated alongside ongoing construction activities.

This visit provided a real-time view of the future of airport mobility, highlighting how integrated planning, construction, and operations come together at a global transportation hub.
